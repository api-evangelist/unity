This summer, Survival Kids launched as a day-one release for Nintendo Switch™ 2. The game was built entirely on Unity 6, marking Unity’s first-ever end-to-end development project, working closely with publisher partner KONAMI.Developing for a new platform on Day 1 is a huge challenge, but the small internal team that built this project included seasoned Unity developers, many of whom have been working in Unity and on games for decades.
