Industrial digital twins are moving beyond visualization toward connecting live machine state, enterprise data, documentation, and operational knowledge into a machine information system. The post argues that across manufacturing, logistics, warehousing, energy, and industrial automation, the digital twin's role is shifting from how realistic the 3D representation looks to how effectively it integrates operational data.
